Coffee Based Items

Espresso Coffee

Recipe of Espresso Coffee

Ingredients:

18-20g ground coffee.

Steps:

1. Fill portafilter with ground coffee.
2. Tamp evenly and lock into espresso machine.
3. Brew for 25-30 seconds. Serve immediately.

Flat White Coffee

Recipe of Espresso Coffee

Ingredients:

1 shot espresso, steamed milk (less foam).

Steps:

1. Brew 1 shot of espresso.
2. Steam milk to a creamy consistency (avoid excess foam).
3. Pour steamed milk over espresso, keeping the texture smooth.

Cappuccino Coffee

Recipe of Cappuccino Coffee

Ingredients:

1 shot espresso, steamed milk, milk foam.

Steps:

1. Brew 1 shot of espresso into a cup.
2. Steam milk to create microfoam.
3. Add equal parts steamed milk and foam to the espresso.

Americano Coffee

Recipe of Americano Coffee

Ingredients:

1 shot espresso, hot water.

Steps:

1. Brew 1 shot of espresso.
2. Add 6-8 ounces of hot water to dilute.
3. Serve immediately.

Cold Brew Coffee

Recipe of Cold Brew Coffee

Ingredients:

Coarsely ground coffee, cold water.

Steps:

1. Combine 1 cup coarsely ground coffee with 4 cups cold water in a jar.
2. Stir, cover, and let it steep in the fridge for 12-24 hours.
3. Strain through a fine mesh or coffee filter.
4. Serve over ice and dilute with water or milk as desired.

Tea Based Items

Black Tea

Recipe of Black Tea

Ingredients:

1 tea bag (Earl Grey or English Breakfast), 8 oz hot water, optional milk or sugar.

Steps:

1. Boil water for 3-5 minutes (212°F/100°C).
2. Steep the tea bag for 3-5 minutes.
3. Remove the tea bag, add milk and/or sugar if desired.

Green Tea

Recipe of Green Tea

Ingredients:

1 green tea bag or 1 tsp loose leaf green tea, 8 oz water (175°F/80°C), optional honey or lemon.

Steps:

1. Boil water for 2-3 minutes (175°F/80°C).
2. Steep for 2-3 minutes.
3. Remove the tea bag or strain the leaves. Add honey or lemon if preferred.

Some More Tea

Matcha Latte

Recipe of Matcha Latte

Ingredients:

1 tsp matcha powder, 2 oz hot water, 6 oz steamed milk, sweetener of choice.

Steps:

1. Boil water for 2-3 minutes.
2. Whisk matcha powder with hot water until smooth.
3. Steam milk and froth it.
4. Combine matcha with steamed milk. Add sweetener to taste

Chai Latte

Recipe of Chai Latte

Ingredients:

1 chai tea bag, 6 oz milk, 8 oz water, sweetener of choice.

Steps:

1. Boil water for 3-5 minutes (212°F/100°C).
2. Steep the chai tea bag for 3-5 minutes.
3. Heat milk separately and froth it.
4. Combine the steeped tea and milk, adding sweetener as desired.

Herbal Teas

Recipe of Herbal Teas

Ingredients:

1 herbal tea bag (chamomile, mint, or rooibos), 8 oz hot water, optional honey or lemon.

Steps:

1. Boil water for 3-5 minutes (212°F/100°C).
2. Steep the tea bag for 5-7 minutes.
3. Remove the tea bag and add honey or lemon if desired.

Iced Tea

Recipe of Iced Tea

Ingredients:

1 tea bag (black, green, or herbal), 8 oz hot water, ice, sweetener, lemon slices or peach syrup.

Steps:

1. Boil water for 3-5 minutes (212°F/100°C).
2. Brew the tea, then cool it down.
3. Add sweetener while the tea is still warm.
4. Pour over ice, then garnish with lemon slices or peach syrup.

Pastries & Bakery Items

Bagels with Cream Cheese or Spreads

Recipe of Bagels

Ingredients:

2 cups flour, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp salt, 1 tbsp yeast, 3/4 cup warm water, 1 tbsp olive oil.

Steps:

1. Mix yeast, warm water, and sugar. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
2. Add flour and salt, knead for 10 minutes.
3. Shape dough into bagels, boil in water for 1-2 minutes, then bake at 375°F for 20-25 minutes.
3. Serve with cream cheese or spreads.

Brownies

Recipe of Brownies

Ingredients:

1/2 cup butter, 1 cup sugar, 2 eggs, 1/3 cup cocoa powder, 1/2 cup flour, 1/4 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp baking powder.

Steps:

1. Melt butter and mix with sugar, eggs, cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder.
2. Bake at 350°F for 20-25 minutes.

Cookies

Recipe of Cookies

Ingredients:

1 cup butter, 3/4 cup sugar, 3/4 cup brown sugar, 2 eggs, 2 1/4 cups flour, 1 tsp baking soda, 1 tsp vanilla, 2 cups chocolate chips.

Steps:

1. Cream butter and sugars, add eggs and vanilla.
2. Mix flour, baking soda, and chocolate chips. Drop spoonfuls on a baking sheet.
3. Bake at 350°F for 8-10 minutes.

Croissants

Recipe of Croissants

Ingredients:

2 cups flour, 1 tsp salt, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tbsp yeast, 1/2 cup butter (cold), 1/2 cup warm water, 1 egg (for glazing).

Steps:

1. Mix yeast, water, sugar, and flour. Knead into dough.
2. Roll dough with cold butter inside, fold and roll to create layers.
3. Shape into croissants, brush with egg wash, and bake at 375°F for 15-20 minutes.

Danishes

Recipe of Danishes

Ingredients:

1 sheet puff pastry, 1/2 cup raspberry jam, 1 egg (for glazing), Powdered sugar.

Steps:

1. Cut puff pastry into squares, add a spoonful of raspberry jam in the center.
2. Fold the corners, brush with egg wash, bake at 375°F for 15-20 minutes.
3. Dust with powdered sugar.

Muffins

Recipe of Muffins

Ingredients:

1 1/2 cups flour. 3/4 cup sugar. 1 tbsp baking powder. 1/2 cup milk. 1/2 cup butter. 1 egg. 1 cup blueberries.

Steps:

1. Mix dry ingredients. Add milk, butter, and egg.
2. Fold in blueberries.
3. Bake at 375°F for 18-20 minutes.

Scones

Recipe of Scones

Ingredients:

2 cups flour, 1/4 cup sugar, 1 tbsp baking powder, 1/2 cup butter, 1/2 cup cream, 1 egg, 1/2 cup dried cranberries.

Steps:

1. Mix dry ingredients, add butter, cream, and egg.
2. Fold in cranberries and form dough into a circle.
3. Cut into wedges and bake at 375°F for 15-20 minutes.

Other Beverages Items

FreshJuices

Recipe of Fresh Juices

Ingredients:

4-5 fresh oranges, 1 tsp sugar (optional).

Steps:

1. Peel and segment the oranges.
2. Blend or juice the oranges.
3. Optionally, add sugar and stir.

Hot Chocolate

Recipe of Hot Chocolate

Ingredients:

2 cups milk, 2 tbsp cocoa powder, 2 tbsp sugar, 1/4 tsp vanilla extract, Whipped cream (optional).

Steps:

1. Heat milk in a pan.
2. Mix cocoa powder and sugar in a separate bowl.
3. Stir cocoa mixture into the hot milk, whisk until smooth.
4. Add vanilla and top with whipped cream if desired.

Lemonade

Recipe of Lemonade

1 cup fresh lemon juice, 4 cups water, 1/2 cup sugar.

Smoothies

Recipe of Smoothies

Ingredients:

1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries), 1/2 banana, 1 cup yogurt or milk, 1 tbsp honey (optional).

Steps:

1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
2. Serve chilled.
